Aug 31 – Sep 7

Sicily & the Aeolian Islands

Mon Aug 31 In transit — overnight flight from Canada

Overnight flight from Canada. Most of this day is in the air — no activities planned.

Tue Sep 1 Land CTA → drive to Milazzo

Land at Catania (CTA) in the morning, collect the rental car, drive straight to Milazzo (~2h north). Keep it easy: short walk up to the Castello di Milazzo for views of the Aeolians on the horizon. Espresso at Bar San Filippo on the seafront. Early dinner, early night — hydrofoil to Panarea tomorrow morning.

Lodging: Hotel Riviera Lido — Mariagrazia's rec, breakfast right on the beach.
Wed Sep 2 Milazzo → Panarea

Morning hydrofoil Milazzo → Panarea (Liberty Lines, ~1h30). Whitewashed houses, black-rock coves, no cars. Hike to Cala Junco and the Bronze Age village ruins at Punta Milazzese. Dinner at Hyccara.

Lodging: ✅ Eunymos Panarea — €184/night (booked).
Thu Sep 3 Panarea → Stromboli + Sciara del Fuoco

Morning hydrofoil Panarea → Stromboli (~30 min). Beach at Ficogrande (black volcanic sand). Sunset: boat tour to the Sciara del Fuoco (~€30/pp, ~3h) — watch lava cascade into the sea. Società Navigazione Pippo. The definitive Stromboli experience.

Lodging: ✅ Hotel Miramare — €150/night (booked).
Fri Sep 4 Stromboli → Vulcano (MG & dad head home)

Optional morning: guided Magmatrek summit hike (book 1+ month ahead). MG and dad take their ferry home. Afternoon hydrofoil Stromboli → Vulcano (~1h30 via Lipari). Evening: sulfur mud baths, swim at Spiaggia dell'Asino, aperitivo at the port.

Lodging: Therasia Resort — confirm booking.
Sat Sep 5 Vulcano → Milazzo → Etna winery

Optional early morning: Gran Cratere hike before the hydrofoil. Drive south to Palmento Costanzo on Etna's northern slopes — 2-night winery stay.

Lodging: Palmento Costanzo ✓ Booked.
Sun Sep 6 Gole dell'Alcantara + Taormina

Morning: Drive down from the winery to the Gole dell'Alcantara (~15 min) — dramatic basalt lava gorges carved by the Alcantara River, walls up to 25m high. Walk into the gorge, wade in the cool turquoise water. Go early before crowds. Bring water shoes — cold and slippery.

Afternoon: Continue to Taormina (~20 min from the gorges) — Teatro Greco with the volcano backdrop (book tickets ahead). Corso Umberto, granita stop, swim at Isola Bella. Return to winery for sunset dinner.

Lodging: Palmento Costanzo ✓ Booked.
Mon Sep 7 🎉 Isabella's engagement party — Catania

Drive to family (~45 min). Full day with family. Engagement party this evening.

Lodging: Grand Hotel Villa Itria.

Sep 8 – Sep 10

Cefalù · Palermo · Scopello

Tue Sep 8 Drive to Cefalù

Drive northwest from Catania (~2h). Norman Duomo (Byzantine mosaics), the Lavatoio medievale, climb La Rocca for views. Sunset stroll along Cefalù beach.

Lodging: Hotel Kalura (family-run, own bay, pool) or Hotel Le Calette (splurge, private cove).
Wed Sep 9 Drive to Palermo · full city day

Drive west (~1h). Vucciria and Ballarò markets, Cattedrale di Palermo, Cappella Palatina. Lunch at Spinnato. Dinner: Ferramenta — stylish trattoria in a converted hardware store near Piazza Olivella.

Lodging: La Terrazza B&B (laterrazzapalace.com).
Thu Sep 10 Scopello / Riserva dello Zingaro hike

Day trip west (~1h) to Scopello — tonnara cove, turquoise water, dramatic rock stacks. Hike the Riserva Naturale dello Zingaro — 7km coastal trail, hidden coves. Bring 2L+ water, trail shoes, snorkeling gear. Early night before morning flight.

Sep 11 – Sep 13

Florence

Fri Sep 11 Fly Palermo → Florence · arrive & explore

Morning flight PMO → Florence. Land, drop bags at the apartment. Walk straight into the historic center: Ponte Vecchio, then Piazza della Signoria and the Loggia dei Lanzi — an open-air sculpture gallery with Roman and Renaissance masterpieces, entirely free. Coffee at Gilli or Paszkowski on Piazza della Repubblica. Late afternoon: walk up to Piazzale Michelangelo for the best panoramic view in Florence. If there's energy: San Miniato al Monte just above, quieter and even more beautiful. Dinner: Trattoria Cammillo or Buca Mario.

Lodging: B52 With a View, Oltrarno ✓ Paid · CAD $1,480.71
Sat Sep 12 Florence at your own pace

Morning: Brunelleschi's Cupola — 463 steps up through the narrow passage between the inner and outer shells of the dome, Vasari's frescoes inches above your head, 360° views of Florence from the lantern at the top. Book a timed slot at duomo.firenze.it (~€30 Opera del Duomo pass). Then down to San Lorenzo market for leather, ceramics, and the covered food market upstairs. Lunch: All'Antico Vinaio on Via dei Neri — Florence's most famous sandwich, worth the short queue. Afternoon: deep into Oltrarno — Piazza Santo Spirito, leather workshops on Borgo San Jacopo, bookbinding shops, Bardini Gardens for a hilltop view without the Boboli crowds. Aperitivo: La Terrazza rooftop of Hotel Continentale — best view of Ponte Vecchio in the city. Early dinner, early night. 🎂

Sep 14 – Sep 17

Tuscany Road Trip

Mon Sep 14 Chianti drive · Antinori tasting · Panzano → Castello di Fonterutoli

Pick up rental car. Antinori nel Chianti Classico — Tinaia Tour. Greve in Chianti, Enoteca Falorni. Lunch: Antica Macelleria Cecchini in Panzano (book ahead). Arrive at Castello di Fonterutoli (Mazzei Wine Resort, Castellina in Chianti) by early evening — check in, estate wine tasting, dinner on site or in the medieval hamlet.

Lodging: Castello di Fonterutoli — Mazzei Wine Resort ✓ Booked · €271.52. us.mazzei.it
Tue Sep 15 Siena morning → Montalcino → Castello Banfi

Drive to Siena (~20 min from Fonterutoli) — Duomo, Piazza del Campo. Then on to Montalcino for a Brunello di Montalcino tasting at Castello Banfi — hilltop castle, terrace views.

Lodging (3 nights, Sep 15–17): ✅ Corte Francigena — CAD $782.33 total (paid).
Wed Sep 16 Val d'Orcia — the Gladiator landscape

Ridley Scott filmed Maximus running through the wheat fields here. The Val d'Orcia in September: golden light, rolling hills, lone cypress trees, absolute silence. Drive slowly. Stop often.

Pienza — Corso Rossellino, Duomo terrace views, pecorino. Lunch: Latte di Luna. Drive the SP146 — pull over at the Podere Belvedere viewpoint. Optional: Abbazia di Sant'Antimo.

Thu Sep 17 Montepulciano · Porta di Bacco · recharge afternoon

Montepulciano — medieval hilltop city, home of Vino Nobile. Browse the medieval corso, visit Piazza Grande, drop into a cellar for a tasting. Lunch: Porta di Bacco (Pookie's pick — reserve ahead). Afternoon: no agenda — wander, pool, aperitivo in town, early dinner. Wind-down day before Rome.

Lodging: ✅ Corte Francigena (3rd night of stay — Sep 15–17, CAD $782.33 total).

Sep 18 – Sep 21

Rome

Fri Sep 18 Saturnia hot springs en route · arrive Rome · Pantheon · Trevi

Leave Montepulciano ~9am. Drive southwest to Saturnia (~1h15) and spend the morning at Cascate del Mulino — free natural hot spring waterfall pools, turquoise thermal water cascading over white travertine terraces. Arrive early before crowds build. Bring water shoes (travertine is very slippery). Leave by 1pm, drive to Rome (~2.5h) — in the city by 3:30–4pm. Pantheon, Trevi Fountain (after 9pm when lit and quieter), Piazza Navona aperitivo. Dinner: Armando al Pantheon or Roscioli.

Lodging (Sep 18–21): Terlie Tomacelli, Via Tomacelli 103 ✓ Paid · CAD $1,112.68
Sat Sep 19 Colosseum · Forum · Palatine

Colosseum + Roman Forum + Palatine with underground/arena floor tour (~€80/pp — book months ahead). Lunch in Monti. Afternoon: Capitoline Museums or a Caravaggio walk. Dinner: Salumeria Roscioli.

Sun Sep 20 Vatican · Sistine · Trastevere

Vatican Museums + Sistine Chapel + St. Peter's — earliest entry slot. Lunch: Bonci Pizzarium. Afternoon: Trastevere, gelato at Fior di Luna. Dinner: Pierluigi or Felice a Testaccio.

Mon Sep 21 Fly home from FCO

Transfer to Fiumicino. Final espresso. Arrivederci, Italia.

Practical Notes

Good to Know

☀️ Weather

28–32°C in Sicily & Rome, low 30s Tuscany. Sea at its warmest; far less crowded than August.

🚗 Driving

Rental car Sep 1 (CTA) for Milazzo. Pick up again Sep 14 Florence → drop Sep 18 near Saturnia or Rome.

🚧 ZTLs

Florence, Rome, Siena, Montepulciano, Pienza all have restricted zones. Get your hotel to register your plate.

♨️ Saturnia

Cascate del Mulino is free. Bring water shoes — travertine is very slippery. Arrive by 9:30–10am (Sep 18) before crowds build; leave by 1pm to reach Rome by mid-afternoon.

🌿 Zingaro Reserve

No food or drink sold inside. Bring 2L+ water each, snacks, trail shoes, snorkeling gear.

💶 Cash

~€200 for Aeolian boat tours, small trattorias, and Sicilian street food.
